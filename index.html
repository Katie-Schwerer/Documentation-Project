    Gender is a text-based game created as a final project for ANT 2420 Gender, Race, and Class. The purpose of the game is to inform the player about issues related to gender. During the game, the player plays as themselves while driving their own story. Depending on the player's race, age, gender, and sexuality, each storyline tackles a difficult issue surrounding gender. Anyone can play this game and learn from these story lines.
