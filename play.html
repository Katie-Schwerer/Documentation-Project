    • How to Play


    1. Download BlueJ
    2. Open BlueJ
    3. Navigate to the Project tab


    4. Click New Project


    5. Name your project, Select the location of the Gender folder you downloaded from Github and click Ok button.


      6. If you did that correctly, you should see this window on your screen.



    6. Right-click on the Main rectangle and click void main(String[] args).


    7. A box will pop up and it will look like this


      8. Just press ok.


    8. Anwser the questions on the window that pops up. The textbox at the bottom of your screen will record your answer as you type.


      9. The rest of the game is typing your answers as you read the screen. Make sure once you finish typing your answer, press the enter key on your keyboard.
